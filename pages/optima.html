Highlights of the design process

A rich and interdisciplinary experience

One of the aspects that I enjoyed the most in this project was that I was working in an agile team, where software engineers, psychologists, a graphic designer and I worked together.

This was my first practical experience with Activity Centered Design and Modeling. I introduced the framework in this project to scope, analyze and design use cases.

Example of use case development and documentation

We had a continuous process of conceptualizing, framing requirements and developing mockups with "Balsamiq mockups" tools before implementation. This was an iterative process where feasibility and desirability where in constant tension.
