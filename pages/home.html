My Design Approach

Be agile but systematic, be concurrent but structured, don't be afraid to experiment and innovate, but confront your assumptions and brightest ideas with reality checks

Within the classical spheres of technology, human factors and economy I combine in a concurrent manner the following design activities.

domain driven design

I analyze the domain in which I design, using the activity modeling framework. This framework has notation that allows to specify with minimum ambiguities requirements and solutions that affect different user segments. It also helps in framing sub-problems and requirements, step by step in a straightforward manner.

value proposition design

Besides understanding the problem at a high level, I also modell user/customer segments, to then identify pains and gains, but also the specific tasks they perform or will perform. This allows to come up with solutions, pain relievers and gains. In this context I frame test two validate new concepts and ideas, in an agile manner, consitent with the lean startup approach.

design for interaction and implementation

This is the most technical work I do, making sure that there is a choherent implementation of interaction design and UX, to reach the goals and requirements unfolded from Activity and Domain driven design, and value proposition design.Here is where tasks such as task flow analysis, sketching prototyping and testing, as well as front-end development takes place.
